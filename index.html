FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

What technologies do you specialize in?

We specialize in .NET and Angular development, advanced AI solutions (LLM, RAG, AI Agents, Chatbots), cloud platforms (AWS, Azure), and enterprise solutions like ERP and CRM systems.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and requirements. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, mobile apps 6-12 weeks, and enterprise solutions 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, security updates, performance monitoring, and feature enhancements. We provide different support packages to meet your specific needs.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely! We excel at collaborating with existing teams, whether for additional development resources, specialized expertise, or project consultation. We adapt to your workflow and communication preferences.

What is your pricing model?

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price projects, hourly rates for ongoing work, and retainer agreements for long-term partnerships. Pricing is always transparent with detailed breakdowns provided upfront.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we work with clients globally while specializing in the MENA region. Our bilingual team and understanding of international business practices make us an ideal partner for cross-border projects.
